This article will guide you in setting up one or more SmartThings compatible hubs and connect them to your Samsung account and SmartThinsg app.

Make sure that you have already created a Samsung account on the SmartThings app before setting up and connecting a SmartThings-compatible hub.

Aeotec Smart Home Hub

This is a good SmartThings compatible hub option to purchase

  • Open the SmartThings app.

  • Click + on the top right of the screen to add a device

  • Click "Add device"


  • Open the "Search Box" by clicking the "Search Icon" on the top right of the screen

  • Search using the "Hub Brand and Model"

  • Click the Name and Model on the search result list


  • SmartThings app confirms it is ready to get started

  • Follow all of the steps in the app

  • Select your "Country"


  • Choose how to connect the hub: Ethernet or Wi-Fi. Both will work

  • Connect the hub to the power outlet


  • Click "Next" if the light is blinking red and green

  • SmartThings app will ask to join your Wi-Fi network - Click "Join"


  • Select the correct Wi-Fi network from the list

  • Patience as the "Hub" is connecting to the internet...


  • Software & Firmware updates will download and be installed if available

  • Yeah. Success! Your "Hub" is now connected. SmartThings assigns a default name and room to the hub


Critical: you are not done yet!

Please do the following 3 things right now:

  1. Device Name: Rename the Hub with a descriptive name of its location on the property. Ex. Hub - 1st Floor North

  2. Location: Make sure the "Location" name is the name of the property. Ex. Aspen Alpine Lodge

  3. Room: Choose the right "Type" of room on the property where the hub is located. If it is not on the list, create a new "Type". Ex. Hallway

This will make it easier to keep tabs on where any devices are and what hubs they are connected to if any issue comes up in the future.
