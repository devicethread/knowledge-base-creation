1- How to create a Samsung account on the SmartThings app

This calls out step by step how to create a Samsung Account on the SmartThings app, which is required to connect hubs and any other device to Devicethread.

  • Download the SmartThings app from the Apple App Store or the Google App Store
    • Apple App Store
    • Google App Store
  • Create a Samsung account on the SmartThings app
  • Click "Sign In"
  • Click "Create account" at the bottom of the page
  • Select all the options by filling all of the check boxes- this will enable Devicethread to perform at its best and enable to control all of the connected devices
  • Fill out your personal information and create a Password
  • Verify you account- an email will be sent to the account email for verification
  • Click "Continue"
  • Congratulations you are done!

  • Please make sure to keep your new Samsung account credentials in a safe place. You will need it when you connect this account to Devicehread.
